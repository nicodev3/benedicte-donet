**Discover the Masterclass « Cultivating Love »**

At the heart of our being, nothing grows with hatred and rejection. That is why it is essential to explore the art of cultivating self-love. Just as you may have learned to judge and reject yourself, you also have the possibility to relearn how to nourish love for the wonderful being that you are.

We all know how important it is to love and appreciate ourselves, but strangely, no one ever gave us the instruction manual!

In this online masterclass, which you can follow from home and at your own pace, I will guide you step by step to help you:

- **Understand what love truly is** and explore your own definition

- **Develop and learn to cultivate this self-love** in your own way

- **Understand your relationship with love**

- Embody and **practice self-love** in different ways.

- **Support materials**: To enrich your experience, you will receive a detailed workbook, writing exercises, as well as a meditation / visualization giving you the tools and techniques to nourish self-love.

 

🌸 « Cultivating Love » is the programme designed to support and guide you on this journey toward yourself, as you truly are.
