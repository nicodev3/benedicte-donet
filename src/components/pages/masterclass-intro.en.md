## YOUR ACCESS TO EXCLUSIVE CONTENT ON MINDFULNESS, SEXUALITY, AND THE NERVOUS SYSTEM.

This part of my offering is **the psycho-education course you never received**. We learn far too little about our psychology, our nervous system, and our sexuality at school.  
There is essential knowledge for better understanding ourselves:

**Understanding our patterns of functioning**,

**Our needs as human beings**,

**Our memory, our traumas**.  
  
Here, I am delighted to share with you what I consider essential for accepting yourself and living in harmony with yourself. These are practices, techniques, and inner explorations that you can follow at home, at your own pace.  
  
I am committed to passing on the teachings that help me and help my patients move towards a more harmonious life.
